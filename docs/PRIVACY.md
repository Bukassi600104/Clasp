# Privacy Policy — Clasp Escrow

_Last updated: launch._

Clasp collects the minimum data needed to operate a non-custodial escrow.

## What we collect
- **Pi identity:** your Pi `uid` and `username`, obtained only through Pi
  Authentication when you sign in.
- **Trade metadata:** amounts (in Pi), descriptions, windows, deadlines, state,
  and the on-chain transaction references for your trades.
- **Dispute evidence:** images and notes you choose to upload during a dispute,
  stored in Firebase Storage.

## What we never collect
- No private keys or wallet passphrases — ever. All signing happens in Pi Wallet.
- No email, password, phone number, or fiat/payment-card data.
- No Pi market-value or price data.

## How it is used
Solely to display trade state, send deadline reminders and notifications, and
compute reputation. We never sell data. The secret Pi Platform API key is used
server-side only and is never exposed to the client.

## Retention & deletion
Trade records persist for dispute integrity and on-chain consistency. You may
request deletion of off-chain metadata (notifications, evidence) by contacting
the operator. On-chain records are immutable by nature.
